Where Beauty

Bridal Studio was born from a single conviction — that every woman deserves to feel like the most breathtaking version of herself, not just on her wedding day, but every single day.

Founded in 2014 by master makeup artist Priya Sharma, our studio has grown from a boutique space into the city's most sought-after luxury beauty destination. We've styled brides, actresses, and women from all walks of life — and the philosophy remains the same: technique meets intuition, luxury meets heart.
